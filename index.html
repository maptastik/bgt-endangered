About the map

This map came out of a one question I had after reading through BGT's list of endangered sites in Central Kentucky: Where are these places? As a geographer and GISer, I frequently think in terms of addresses and coordinates. While the initial list offered some clues to the locations of these endangered sites, there was a lot of variability in the scale of precision.

In making this map I've sought to get a little closer to plotting the locations of what I've come to learn are some historically and culturally valuable artifacts of the regions built environment. While I have tried to locate these sites accurately, I admit and fully expect that there is much room for improvement. If you see a location that is incorrectly mapped, please let me know!
